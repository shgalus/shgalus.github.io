Generates observations on a normal hidden Markov model.

Generates T observations y and X on a normal hidden Markov model with transition matrix P, initial state distribution p, vector of means at each state mu and vector of standard deviations at each state sigma. Uses the generator g. The vectors y and X are resized in this function.
