117  /**
118  * Random number from logarithmic series distribution.
119  * Logarithmic series distribution with parameter 0 < p < 1 has
120  * the probability function \f[ \Pr(X = i) = (a / i)p^i, \; i =
121  * 1, 2, 3, \ldots, \; a = -\frac{1}{\log(1 - p)}.\f] The
122  * expected value and variance are \f[ EX = ap / (1 - p), \; D^2X
123  * = ap(1 - ap) / (1 - p)^2. \f]
124  *
125  * \exception SHG::Invalid_argument unless p > 0 and p < 1.0
126  *

128  * std::numeric_limits<unsigned long>::max(), what may happen for
129  * p close to 1
130  *
131  * \implementation Kemp's second accelerated generator,
